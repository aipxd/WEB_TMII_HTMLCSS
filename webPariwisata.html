Taman Mini Indonesia Indah (TMII) is a recreational and cultural park located in East Jakarta, showcasing the diverse culture, architecture, and traditions of all provinces in Indonesia. Within TMII, visitors can see miniature traditional houses, museums, gardens, and various educational and entertainment attractions reflecting the nation's rich culture. This park is a favorite destination for family outings, education, and the preservation of Indonesian culture.
